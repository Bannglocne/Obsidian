#thermodynamics #physics #principles #energy 
> **Internal energy**, $E$, of a system as the sum of all the [[kinetic energy]] and [[potential energy]] of the components of the system.

$$
\Delta E = R_{final} - E_{initial}
$$
If
1. $\Delta E > 0$: System gain [[energy]] from it surroundings
2. $\Delta E < 0$: System has lost energy to its surroundings.

=> $\Delta E = q + w$ 

| For $q$        | + means system gains [[heat]]            | - means system loses [[heat]]            |
| -------------- | ---------------------------------------- | ---------------------------------------- |
| For $w$        | + means [[work]] done on the system      | - means [[work]] done by the system      |
| For $\Delta E$ | + means net gain of [[energy]] by system | - means net loss of [[energy]] by system |
