#dynamics #physics 
> **Friction** is a resistive [[force]] opposing [[motion]] or its tendency, opposes relative [[motion]] between systems in contact..

If two systems are in contact and stationary / moving relative to one another, then the friction between them is called **static friction** / **kinetic friction**. 

The magnitude of static friction $f_s$ is $$f_s \leqslant \mu_s N$$ 

The magnitude of kinetic friction $f_k$ is given by $$f_k = \mu_k N$$ 

where $\mu_s$ is the coefficient of static friction and $N$ is the magnitude of the normal [[force]]
