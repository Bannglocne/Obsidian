#quantum #physics #electricity
$$
\text{Energy of photon} = E = hv
$$
Thus, radiant energy itself is [[quantized]].

> Under the right conditions, photons striking a metal surface can transfer their [[energy]] to electrons in the metal.


> [!NOTE] 
> A certain amount of [[energy]]—called the work [[function]]—is required for the electrons to overcome the attractive forces holding them in the metal.
