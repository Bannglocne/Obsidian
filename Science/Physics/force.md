#physics #dynamics
> An intuitive definition of **force**—that is, a push or a pull (has both magnitude and direction).


> [!NOTE] SI unit of force: Newton (N)
> $1$N is the force needed to accelerate an object with a mass of $1$kg at a rate of 1$m/s^2$

The **net external force $\vec{F}_{\text{net}}$**: $$\vec{F}_{\text{net}} = \Sigma \vec{F} = \vec{F}_1 + \vec{F}_2 + ...$$
