#orbital #chemistry #physics #quantum 
> Each [[orbital]] is denoted by a box and each electron by a half arrow. A half arrow pointing up/down  represents an electron with a positive/negative [[electron spin|spin]] magnetic [[quantum numbers|quantum number]]. 

$$
\text{Li} \quad
\begin{array}{c}
\boxed{\textcolor{red}{\uparrow \downarrow}} \\
1s
\end{array}
\quad
\begin{array}{c}
\boxed{\textcolor{red}{\uparrow}\phantom{\downarrow}} \\
2s
\end{array}
$$
