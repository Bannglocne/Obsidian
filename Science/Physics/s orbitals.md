[[radial probability function|Radial probability distributions]] for the 1s, 2s, and 3s [[orbital|orbitals]] of hydrogen.

There trends:
1. The number of peaks increases with increasing n, with the outermost peak being larger than inner ones. 
2. The number of nodes increases with increasing n. 
3. The electron density becomes more spread out with increasing n.
