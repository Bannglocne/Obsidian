> The [[heat capacity]] of one gram of a substance is called its **specific heat capacity**, or merely its **specific heat**.

$$
\text{Specific heat} = \frac{\text{(quantity of heat transferred)}}{\text{(grams of substance)} \times \text{(temperature change)}}
$$

$$
C_s = \frac{q}{m \times \Delta T}
$$
