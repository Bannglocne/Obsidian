> The [[enthalpy]] change that accompanies a reaction is called either the **enthalpy of reaction** or **heat of reaction** and is sometimes written $\Delta H_{rxn}$.

- $\Delta H_{rxn} < 0$: The process is [[endothermic]]
- $\Delta H_{rxn} > 0$: The process is [[exothermic]]
