> The **standard enthalpy of formation** of a compound, $\Delta H^{\circ}_f$ , is the change in [[enthalpy]] for the reaction that forms one [[Avogadro's number and the mole|mole]] of the compound from its elements with all substances in their [[standard  state|standard states]].

If: elements (in standard state) -> compound (1 mol in [[standard  state]]])
Then: $\Delta H$ = $\Delta H^{\circ}_f$
