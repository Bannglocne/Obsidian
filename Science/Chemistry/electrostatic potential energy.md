One of the most important forms of [[potential energy]] in chemistry is **electrostatic potential energy**, $E_{el}$, which arises from the interactions between charged particles.

$$
E_{el} = \frac{KQ_1Q_2}{d}
$$
here $k$ is simply a constant of proportionality, $8.99 \times 10^9 J-m/C^2$
