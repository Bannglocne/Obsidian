> Beginning with $Sc$ and extending through $Zn$, electrons are added to the five 3d [[d orbitals|orbitals]] until they are com pletely filled.

In writing the electron configurations of the **transition elements**, we fill orbitals in accordance with [[Hund's rule]]—we add them to the 3d [[d orbitals|orbitals]] singly until all five or bitals have one electron each and then place additional electrons in the 3d orbitals with [[electron spin|spin]] pairing until the shell is completely filled.
