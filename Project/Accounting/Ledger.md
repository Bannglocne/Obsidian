#economics #accounting 
> The entire group of [[Account|accounts]] maintained by a company is the **ledger**. 
> The **ledger** provides the balance in each of the [[Account|accounts]] as well as keeps track of changes in these balances.

---
# General Ledger

| Individual Asset Accounts | Individual Liability Accounts | Individual Owner's Equity Accounts |
| ------------------------- | ----------------------------- | ---------------------------------- |
| Equipment                 | Interest Payable              | Salaries and Wages Expense         |
| Land                      | Salaries and Wages Payable    | Service Revenue                    |
| Supplies                  | Accounts Payable              | Owner's Drawings                   |
| Cash                      | Notes Payable                 | Owner's Capital                    |
