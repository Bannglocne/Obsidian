#economics 
>  To evaluate market outcomes, economists use **Total Surplus** as a measure of a society's economic well-being.

$$
\text{Total Surplus} = \text{Value to buyers } – \text{ Cost to sellers}
$$

![[Consumer_Producer_Surplus.png]]
1. Free [[market|markets]] allocate the [[supply]] of goods to the buyers who value them most highly, as measured by their [[willingness to pay]]. 
2. Free [[market|markets]] allocate the [[demand]] for goods to the sellers who can produce them at the lowest cost.
3. Free [[market|markets]] produce the quantity of goods that maximizes the sum of [[Consumer Surplus|consumer]] and [[producer surplus]].
