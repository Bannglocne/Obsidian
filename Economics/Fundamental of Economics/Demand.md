# Shifts in the Demand Curve
![[Shifts_in_the_Demand_Curve.png]]
While a change in price causes a movement _along_ the curve, other factors can shift the entire curve:
1. **Income**: For [[Normal Good]] , demand increases with income; for [[Inferior Good]], demand decreases as income rises.
2. **Prices of Related Goods**: Demand for a good can be affected by the price of [[Substitutes]] or [[Complements]].
