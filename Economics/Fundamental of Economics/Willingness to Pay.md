#economics 
> Each buyer’s maximum is called his **willingness to pay**, and it measures how much that buyer values the good.

==Example==:

| Buyer | Willingness to Pay |
| ----- | ------------------ |
| A     | $100               |
| B     | 80                 |
| C     | 70                 |
| D     | 50                 | 

> A is willing to pay $100 for the good but pays only $80 for it. We say that A receives [[Consumer Surplus]] of $20.
