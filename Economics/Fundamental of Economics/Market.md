# The Market for Loanable Funds
> **Market for loanable funds**: The market in which those who want to save supply funds and those who want to borrow to invest demand funds
Economists use a supply-and-demand model to explain how the [[Financial System]] works.
- **Supply:** Comes from national saving. 
- **Demand:** Comes from households and firms wanting to borrow for investment.
