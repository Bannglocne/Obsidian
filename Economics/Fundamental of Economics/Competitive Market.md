#economics 
> **Competitive market** is a [[market]] in which there are many buyers and many sellers so that each has a negligible impact on the market price.

| Feature           | **Perfect Competition** | **Monopolistic Comp.** | **Oligopoly**     | **Monopoly** |
| --------------------- | ----------------------- | ---------------------- | ----------------- | ------------ |
| Number of Sellers | Many                    | Many                   | Few               | One          |
| Product           | Identical               | Differentiated         | Similar/Identical | Unique       |
| Price Influence   | None                    | Some                   | Significant       | Total        |
| Barriers to Entry | None                    | Low                    | High              | Very High    |
