# International Trade
Economists compare the domestic price to the **world price**  to determine trade patterns.

 =>Restricting Trade or Not?

| Benefits from International Trade       | Arguments for Trade Restrictions                                                                               |
| --------------------------------------- | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Increased variety of goods.             | Destroys domestic jobs in industries that cannot compete                                                       |
| Lower costs through economies of scale. | Key industries (like steel for weapons) must be protected to ensure self-sufficiency during war.               | 
| Enhanced flow of ideas.                 | New industries need temporary protection to grow and become competitive.                                       |
| Increased competition.                  | Free trade is only desirable if all countries follow the same rules                                            |
|                                         | Trade restrictions can be used as a threat to help remove restrictions already imposed by foreign governments. |
