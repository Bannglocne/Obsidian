#marketing #economics 
> 1. Customers form expectations about the _value_ and _satisfaction_ that various [[market offerings]] will deliver and buy accordingly. 
> 2. Marketers must be careful to set the _right level of expectations_. 
> - If they set expectations too low, they may satisfy those who buy but fail to attract enough buyers. 
> - If they set expec tations too high, buyers will be disappointed.
