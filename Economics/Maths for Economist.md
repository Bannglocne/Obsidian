- ==Elasticity Rule==: The more elastic (responsive) the supply or demand curve, the larger the deadweight loss.
	- Logic: When participants are highly responsive to price changes, they exit the market more easily in response to a tax, causing the market quantity to drop significantly and resulting in a larger loss of surplus.
