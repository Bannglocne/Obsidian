#economics #financial-market 
> A **security (==Chứng khoán==)** (also called a _financial instrument_) is a claim on the issuer’s future income or assets (any financial claim or piece of property that is subject to owner ship)

Securities are divided into three main types:
1. **Equity securities**: Represent ownership rights (e.g., [[Stock|Stocks]]).
2. **Debt securities**: Money you lend to an organization and they pay interest (e.g., [[Bond|Bonds]]).
3. **Derivatives**: Financial contracts whose value is based on another underlying asset (e.g., Options, Futures).
