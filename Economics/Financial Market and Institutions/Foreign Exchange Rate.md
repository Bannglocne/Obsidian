> **Foreign exchange rate (==Tỷ giá đối hoái==)**: The price of one country’s currency in terms of another’s.

![[Exchange_Rate_of_the_US_Dollar.png]]
- _A weaker dollar_ leads to more expensive foreign goods, Americans decrease their purchases of foreign goods and increase their consumption of domes tic goods
- _A strong dollar_ benefited American consumers by making foreign goods cheaper but hurt American businesses and eliminated some jobs by cutting both domestic and foreign sales of their products.
